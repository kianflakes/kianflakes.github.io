Born in Barcelona, Spain. I entered ECIB film school in 2018 where I earned a diploma in Cinematography.

Though my time there I developed a passion for 1st and 2nd ACing, I was passionate about being around cameras, working along side the DP to bring creative and technical
solutions to his camera needs as well as assisting other focus pullers, lifting as many burdens off their shoulders and being as efficient as possible.

I've done work in music videos, commercials and some student short films, I look forward to working in features and documentaries in the future as I keep learning the art
of focus pulling and 2nd Assisting.
