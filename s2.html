Shot and edited by Kian Rahnema @ ECIB, Barcelona. (2019)
Trying to light and block a scene similar to the interrogation scene in Memories of Murder (2003). To complish that two 120cm KinoFlo lights were used, one to light the subjects and one to light the background.
The phisical size of the KinoFlo helped light all the subjects while it's soft output made the falloff at a close distance noticeable, thus achieving different exposures on the faces of the talents.


technical: Blackmagic Production 4k + Samyang EF Lenses, 2x 120cm KinoFlo.
